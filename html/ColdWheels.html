About the Game

Cold Wheels is an adrenaline-pumping arcade racing game where speed, style, and daring stunts are your ticket to victory. Set in a frozen, post-apocalyptic world where icy roads, high-tech vehicles, and relentless competition collide, you'll race across treacherous terrains, unlock powerful upgrades, and outsmart your rivals in a series of high-octane challenges. It’s not just about finishing first—it’s about looking good while doing it.

Key Features

  • Fast-Paced Arcade Racing: Experience the thrill of high-speed, gravity-defying races across the tracks. Drift, boost, and barrel-roll your way to the finish line.
  • Epic Winter Environments: Race through a stunning variety of frozen, snow-covered landscapes—icy mountain passes, frozen cities, frosty wastelands, and high-speed ice tunnels.
  • Customizable Cars: Build your dream machine from scratch with a wide selection of futuristic vehicles, customizable body kits, engine parts, and performance upgrades.
  • High-Energy Power-ups: Pick up power-ups scattered throughout each race and use them to gain the upper hand against your opponents.
  • Challenging Career Mode: Dive into a thrilling career mode where you'll unlock new tracks, vehicles, and upgrades as you climb through the ranks.
