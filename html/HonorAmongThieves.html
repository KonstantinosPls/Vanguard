About the Game

Dragons & Dungeons: Honor Among Thieves is an action-packed RPG set in the mysterious and mythical world of Eldria. As the realm teeters on the brink of destruction, ancient forces stir, and heroes rise from every corner of the land. Players will explore vast dungeons, battle mighty dragons, and forge alliances in their quest to save the world from an ancient evil.

Key Features

  • Epic Storyline: An immersive story of heroes, dragons, and dark forces.
  • Dragon Battles: Engage in fierce battles with legendary dragons.
  • Co-op Multiplayer: Team up with friends or players online for cooperative quests.
  • Character Customization: Build your hero with diverse classes and unique skills.
  • Open-World Exploration: Explore the vast world of Eldria, from enchanted forests to haunted castles.
