About the Game

Enter the haunting world of Tiles of Fear, a psychological horror experience that plunges you into the depths of a crumbling, cursed mansion. Navigate eerie corridors and solve twisted puzzles as you uncover the horrifying truth behind its dark history. The mansion shifts and changes, turning every step into a test of courage. Will you confront your fears or succumb to them?

Key Features

  • Dynamic Environment: A constantly changing mansion that warps around you, keeping you on edge.
  • Immersive Atmosphere: Stunning visuals, unsettling sound design, and eerie lighting immerse you in terror.
  • Psychological Tension: Unpredictable scares and a gripping narrative keep you guessing.
  • Complex Puzzles: Solve intricate puzzles that reveal chilling secrets about the mansion's past.
  • Choices Matter: Your decisions shape the story and lead to multiple endings.
