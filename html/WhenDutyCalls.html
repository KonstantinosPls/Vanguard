About the Game

"When Duty Calls" is an adrenaline-pumping singleplayer/multiplayer FPS that challenges players to work as a team in tactical combat scenarios. Set in a near-future dystopia, the game immerses players in high-stakes missions to protect their country from insurgents, rogue states, and global threats. Team up with friends or players worldwide to conquer the battlefield!

Key Features

  • Intense Combat: Engage in realistic, tactical gunfights.
  • Co-op Multiplayer: Work with friends to complete missions or fight against each other in PVP modes.
  • Custom Loadouts: Choose your weapons, gadgets, and abilities for each mission.
  • Dynamic Environments: Battle in diverse maps, from war-torn cities to dense jungles.
  • Rank Up: Level up and unlock powerful rewards and skins.
