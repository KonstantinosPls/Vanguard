GAME OF THE YEAR
Dragons and Dungeons: Honor Among Thieves


1. The D20 Enthusiast
Rating: 4.5/5
“Dragons & Dungeons: Honor Among Thieves is a love letter to everything we hold dear about tabletop RPGs.
The game captures the essence of classic D&D with a few modern twists.
Combat is tactical and engaging, with dice rolls that genuinely feel consequential.
The world-building is superb—each town feels alive, with NPCs that have stories to tell and secrets to reveal.
The campaign itself is a thrilling ride, though it occasionally dips into cliché territory.
Still, the charm and creativity shine through in a way that even long-time D20 veterans can appreciate.
2. The Lore Master
Rating: 3/5
“Visually stunning and lore-rich, Honor Among Thieves will immerse you in a beautifully crafted world.
Though its depth is occasionally undermined by its reliance on tired fantasy tropes.
The ancient dragon gods? Check. The sinister cult threatening the realm? Check.
The rogue bandit who ‘knows more than he lets on’? You guessed it—check.
While there’s a wealth of backstory to explore, much of it feels recycled from a thousand other fantasy campaigns.
